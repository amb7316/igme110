A Little Bit About Me 2, Electric Boogaloo

If you remember the second sentence of my first paragraph, I mentioned that I am a transfer student. "Why did you transfer?", you might ask. Excellent question reader, we'll delve into it right now. I originally attended SUNY University at Buffalo (also called UB, yayyyyy more acronyms) in the fall and spring of 2018. While I attended UB, I had changed my major multiple times and I was still feeling like I couldn't see myself in a fulfilling career after I graduated. I had always had a curiosity for how games were made and I enjoyed used my creativity, so when I was looking for colleges to transfer to, I looked at RIT for it's Game Design and Development program. Combined with the opportunities provided by Co-ops and the pleasant atmosphere, I decided to enroll at RIT. Looking back, the decision to enroll at RIT was an excellent choice, since I'm enjoying my college experience infinitely more as well as getting a respectable education in a field I am passionate about. I hope that this passion keeps burning here at RIT. Go Tigers!
